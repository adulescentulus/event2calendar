Saturday’s Apartment


Back


CATALOGUE TEXT
Power drill, trampoline and shouting: that’s all the inhabitants of the high-rise know of their neighbours. The noise is getting louder and louder and the sufferers more and more annoyed – until there’s a blackout. The sudden quiet brings the tenants together, with unexpected consequences. Now everyone must roll up their sleeves to build a new home together.

Marie-Thérèse Antony
